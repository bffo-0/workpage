About

Tommaso Massimiliano Alfì is a composer and sound designer based in Turin. He has collaborated with various musicians and artists in the fields of music, video art, and cinema. His approach is influenced by his main project, "MVRGN," which explores a contemporary, hybrid, and electronic sound. His latest work is "[threaded or shredded] une fois de plus, sans": a four-channel structure of contemporary composed music and sound design installed for the Gerrit Rietveld Academie.

[threaded or shredded] une fois de plus, sans

on tenter hooks [threaded or shredded] une fois de plus, sans. She uses the plain weave, the strongest, simplest and most ancient of weaving techniques. She occasionally intervenes in it, pulling at the threads, un-evening the pattern and adjusting the shape in a way that is both patient and slightly violent, challenging its pre-determined form. dariya likes to rearrange glossaries, grids and gaps in her close readings of architecture and built environments. She indicates the performative potential of space, working with object-based compositions, voice, sound and movement. Stretching the codes of repetition.
